Most Las Vegas residents know our festival as a monthly event attended by local artists, musicians, producers and many others.

We provide a platform where everyone can celebrate our local culture and creativity in all their forms. We are best known for our monthly arts and culture festival held in the Las Vegas Arts District.

Our many efforts to stimulate creativity and positivity in our community would not have been possible without sponsors, community partners and, of course, our local art community

The festival is held every first Friday of the month in the center of the Las Vegas art district - it's written in white on the photo
